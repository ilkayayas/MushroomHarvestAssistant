Privacy Policy

Mushroom Harvest Assistant · Last updated: May 31, 2026

This app does NOT collect, store, or share your personal data.

Camera & LiDAR

The camera and LiDAR sensor are used only for mushroom measurement and are processed in real time on your device. Images are never uploaded or saved to any server.

No Account / Sign-up

We never ask for your name, email, location, or any identity information. No account is required.

No Analytics / Tracking

Your usage is not tracked. No third-party analytics or advertising networks are used.

Subscriptions

All purchases are handled entirely by the Apple App Store. We have no access to your payment or billing information; Apple’s privacy policy applies to those transactions.

Data Storage & Internet

The app does not exchange data over the internet. The only exception is a one-time connection to Apple’s servers on first launch to decrypt the AI model. This connection contains no personal data.

Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from children — it collects no data from anyone.

Changes

If this policy is updated, the current version will be published on this page.
